    Glenfiddich 12

    Glenfiddich is world famous for its lightness of character. Evoking a sense of green freshness with fruitful scents of green apple and pear. 

    This 12 year old single malt is the world's best selling single malt scotch and has become a benchmark whisky against which all others must be measured. 

    Matured in bourbon and sherry oak casks in one of the Distilleries Dufftown warehouses, the resulting whisky is a well rounded one with subtle notes of fresh pear, vanilla and a generally fruity finish.
